our story

A universe of sacred mountains

Find your verse at the summit of soul.

Six years ago, we met beneath the snow-capped mountains of Daocheng Yading. It was an afternoon touched by sunlight and silence — running across alpine grasslands, laughing freely, the light wrapped around us like a soft blanket. That afternoon became the beginning of everything: love, marriage, a growing family, and a shared longing to return to that feeling of being held by nature.

wool care guide

Cared for, it comes along for years

Wool is a gift from nature. Light surface pilling is part of its character, not a defect — and with a little care it keeps its softness and loft for many journeys.

Daily use & storage
— Minimize rubbing against rough surfaces or pack straps to prevent pilling.
— Before long storage: clean first, fold, keep dry and out of direct sun.
— For creases, use medium steam with a damp cloth between iron and fabric.
Cleaning & washing
— Professional dry cleaning preserves texture best.
— If hand washing: neutral wool detergent, water below 30 °C, 15 minutes max. Never rub, wring or twist.
— Dry flat in cool shade. Avoid machine washing, tumble drying, hot water and direct sun.
